OUR MISSION

At Life After Freedom we want to bridge the gap between formerly incarcerated people and job employment. We aim to show companies how they can improve their company and improve society as whole just by changing someone’s life with a job. Our end goal is to cultivate bonds between companies and formerly incarcerated people that help both parties.

WHAT WE DO

Life After Freedom provides a free job search and matching application for formerrly incarcerated people. We help businesses improve their company and society by helping them understand the benefits of hiring formerly incacerated people.
