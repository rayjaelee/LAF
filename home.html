CULTIVATING NEW CONNECTIONS

Here at Life After Freedom we believe in cultivating new connections with Businesses and Formerly Incarcerated People.
There is a negative stigma over Formerly Incarcerated People that hinders their growth, as well as the growth of our society. By partnering with businesses we are able to improve society as a whole and help erase the negative stigmas over Formerly Incarcerated People. We believe that by helping Formerly Incarcerated People find jobs they can live a better life and help contribute to the advancement of companies and our society!
